Welcome!

Buggg.gs is a database that gives insect collectors and enthusiasts an opportunity to closly examine specimens that they would otherwise not have access to. Our goal is to grow a virtual archive of insects from diverse locations, and of varying shape, size, color, and species. Enjoy!

How do I submit
my own bugs?

Great question. If you are intersted in participating, please send a 300DPI resolution scan to Buggggs@gmail.com. To be of scientific value, all bugs are labeled with the the appropriate location, scientific order, date, and collector.
